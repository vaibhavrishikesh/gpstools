Privacy Policy — Gps Camera Location

App package: com.gpstools.camera · Last updated: 18 June 2026

Gps Camera Location ("the app", "we") is a GPS camera that stamps your photos with their location, address, map, date-time and weather. This policy explains what the app accesses and how that information is used. In short: your photos and location stay on your device — we do not run any server and we do not collect or store your personal data ourselves.

Information the app uses

  • Precise location (GPS/NavIC): used to geotag a photo — the latitude/longitude, address, altitude, compass direction and a map thumbnail are written onto the photo you capture and saved on your device.
  • Camera: used only to take photos within the app.
  • Photos & media: captured photos and exported PDF reports are saved to your device's storage. We do not upload them anywhere.

The app has no account or sign-in and does not ask for your name, email or phone number.

When location leaves your device

To build the on-photo stamp, your coordinates are sent to these services only to fetch information about that location — they are not used to identify you:

  • OpenStreetMap — to fetch the map thumbnail of the area (privacy policy).
  • Open-Meteo — to fetch the current weather at the location (terms).
  • Android / Google geocoder — to convert coordinates into a readable address.

Advertising

The free version shows ads via Google AdMob. Google and its partners may use device identifiers, IP address and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's Advertising privacy notice. You can remove ads with the in-app purchase. We do not receive your personal data from AdMob.

Purchases

Optional "Remove ads" and "Pro" purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details.

Permissions

  • CAMERA — to capture photos.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — to geotag photos with an accurate fix.
  • Storage (older Android versions) — to save photos and reports.

Data retention & your control

Photos and reports remain on your device until you delete them. Revoking the camera or location permission in Android Settings stops that access immediately.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.
